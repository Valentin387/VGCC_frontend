About VGCC Project

VGCC Software, licensed by Valentin Valencia, seamlessly integrates Valentin Google Calendar and ChatGPT services to provide comprehensive assistance with your Google Calendar events.

Key Features:

  • Select multiple Google Calendars and effortlessly query ChatGPT about any details within your agenda.
  • View events from your calendar spanning past, present, and future dates.
  • No personal user information is stored to ensure privacy.

How to Use:

  1. Select the date range of events from your calendar that require assistance.
  2. Click "Add Calendar" and consent to Google's terms.
  3. Utilize the chat interface on the right to inquire about your calendars.
  4. Press "Reset All" to permanently delete all accounts and chat history.

Examples of Use:

  1. Include your personal and work calendar emails, then ask, "What do I have planned for the next week? Are there any scheduling conflicts?"
  2. Incorporate calendars of friends and seek advice on the ideal day and time for social gatherings or vacations.

Notes:

  • Calendars added are valid for only 24 hours for security reasons.
  • The project is currently intended for local usage and is not recommended for deployment.
  • OpenAI API is utilized, and they enforce their own personal data policy.
  • VGCC stands for Valentin Google Calendar + ChatGPT integration.
